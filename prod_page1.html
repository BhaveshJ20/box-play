A deeper look
into the course

Understand why Active Listening is one of the most critical skills in effective communication. Consider how being analytical in your interactions can reinforce your understanding of a conversation, improve your ability to retain information and accelerate your problem solving.

Learn the proven steps of Active Listening and how to apply them. Practice how to intentionally understand both emotions and facts within conversations, and respond through verbal and non-verbal cues that demonstrate your engagement and comprehension.

Discover the CQ: Communication Quotient Loop and how it can be broken by ineffective listening styles such as Passive, Selective and Competitive. Explore how these ineffective types of listening impact the communication flow, and learn to recognize and avoid their specific traits.

Discover how Active Listening enables work-based relationships to thrive. Recognize how exchanging meaningful feedback can directly improve coordination and team management, building a climate of positive collaboration with both employees and partners.

Use Active Listening to solve conflicts efficiently. Manage and avoid misunderstandings and bias, developing skills to discern what challenges or problems the other person may be facing.
